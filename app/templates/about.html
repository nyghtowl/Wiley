About Us

Wiley & Warrick provides data science and software engineering application development services.

Our services and products include machine learning data science applications, responsive designed website applications. We can add features and functionality that improve application functionality and interaction (e.g. classification and recommendation algorithms, API integration like Twilio, multiple languages, Oauth, etc.).

Additionally we can add back-end solutions to store and leverage data (e.g. Postgres, Redis, MySQL). We can setup website performance and support tools and provide support and coaching for project and operational needs to get the work done.

We've worked with start-ups to Fortune 500 companies spanning diverse industries (e.g. high-tech, retail, bio-tech, utility, etc.). We've worked under a range of budgets across various locals. Basically, we have extensive experience and access to industry resources that ensure well thought out and solid solutions.

We are experts at solving problems and developing solutions especially when there is significant ambiguity. Check out our services section and contact us for more information.
