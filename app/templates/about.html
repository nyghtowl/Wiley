About Us

Wiley & Warrick has extensive experience and access to industry resources that ensure succcessful and quality solutions.

We've worked with start-ups to Fortune 500 companies spanning diverse industries (e.g. high-tech, retail, pharmaceutical, finance, healthcare, non-profit, entertainment, utility and transportation) and budgets that range from a few thousand to multi-millions. We even had experience working internationally (e.g. Kenya, England, Canada, Indonesia, Peru, Bangladesh, etc.).

We are great at software engineering, business analytics, social business software, enterprise content management, strategic planning, project management, and coaching.

We are experts in dealing with ambiguity and getting things done.

Vision

Wiley & Warrick is a women-owned and women-run corporation that believes deeply in solving problems with panache while equalizing gender representation in technology.

Our vision includes quality software and business services and products. We believe in communication -- that communication is linch-pin to successful cient collaboration and making sure our customers get what they need and want. Bottom line, we want to make it easier for you to get the work done and make better decisions.

We are also passionate about increasing female representation in technology and we plan to continue to support efforts to increase participation.
