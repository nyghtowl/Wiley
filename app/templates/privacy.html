Privacy Policy

What We Collect and How It's Used

Sharing Your Information

Unless we have your permission or applicable laws are being enforced, we will not share your sensitive information.

Should Wiley & Warrick ever merge with, acquire, or be sold to another company, you will be notified prior to your personal information being transferred and subjected to a different Privacy Policy.

Security of Your Information

Since you provide us with sensitive information, we take every measure possible to protect your information against unauthorized access or disclosure. Access to your information is restricted to employees or contractors of Wiley & Warrick who are bound by confidentiality obligations and non-disclosure agreements.

Changes
